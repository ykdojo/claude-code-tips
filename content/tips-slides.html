Tip 8: How do I manage my codebase when AI writes a lot of it?

  • If you're an engineering manager, build a shared understanding with your team of how much codebase understanding you expect
  • Sensitive parts that need to be secure and safe → deeper understanding
  • Medium-level projects (e.g. front-end only) → file-level understanding: what each file does and how they interact
  • One-off projects → totally vibe coding might be totally okay
